VOLKSWAGEN

Volkswagen is a German car manufacturer that designs, manufactures, and distributes passenger and commercial vehicles. The company was founded in 1937 by the German Labour Front and operates in 153 countries. Some of Volkswagen's popular cars include the Golf, Jetta, Polo, Beetle, and Passat. Volkswagen is known for designing reliable vehicles that are designed to last for years. Volkswagen has factories in many parts of the world, including Germany, Mexico, United States, Slovakia, China, India, and Russia. The Volkswagen Group in India has two plants, one in Pune and one in Aurangabad. The Pune plant covers an area of over 2.3 million square meters and has a production capacity of up to 200,000 vehicles a year.
