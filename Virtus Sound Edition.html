Know your Virtus Sound edition


Virtus Sound Edition

Take to the roads with your favourite music in your ears, when you’re driving a Virtus Sound Edition, available in Lava Blue, Carbon Steel Grey, Rising Blue and Wild Cherry Red.

Take to the road with remarkable features like:

  • Powered by the legendary TSI Engine
  • Exceptional sound package with subwoofer + amplifier
  • Exciting C-pillar graphics with Sound Edition badge
  • Twin front electric seats



Exteriors,

C-pillar graphics & Sound Edition badge

Distinguish yourself not just with song choice, the Equalizer graphics ring out loudly. Together with the Sound Edition badge, they mark your car as one that is always on beat.

Turbo-charged TSI

While you’re relaxing with your tunes, remember your Taigun has a finely tuned turbo-charged TSI engine at its heart. Maximum power, minimum consumption allows for excellent efficiency on the go.








Interiors,

Twin Front Electric Seats

Move to the rhythm in comfort, with first-in-segment electric seats for the driver and passenger that allow you to choose the best position with a flick of a switch.

New in Sound Edition: Subwoofer

The subwoofer and amplifier combination allows music of your choice to permeate your car. Appreciate every beat as you immerse yourself in a sonic experience.







Safe like a Volkswagen

The Volkswagen Virtus achieved 5 stars in Global NCAP's updated crash tests, becoming one of the first models ever in India to achieve five stars for both adult and child occupants' protection. Ride easy and focus on the music when a safe drive is assured.
